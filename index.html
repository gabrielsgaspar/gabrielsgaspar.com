Welcome to my website!

My name is Gabriel Simões Gaspar and I am an applied microeconomist currently pursuing a PhD in Economics at the London Business School. My research interests lie at the intersection of political economy and development, with a particular emphasis on environmental issues and discrimination.

Prior to my PhD, I have worked at the World Bank and obtained an MSc in Economics from LSE, an MSc in Foreign Service from Georgetown University, and a BA in International Relations from the Catholic University of Brasilia.
